## RKLB: $2.2B backlog + Neutron = two-layer moat now validated

**Claim:** Rocket Lab has a validated two-layer moat — Electron (87 launches, proven small-lift workhorse) generates recurring revenue while Neutron (Q4 2026 first flight, 13,000 kg LEO) targets the megaconstellation market with a $2.2B+ backlog and its largest-ever contract signed May 2026.
**Evidence:** >70 missions in manifest. Q1 2026 sold more launches than all of 2025 combined. May 2026: confidential customer books 5 Neutron + 3 Electron launches for 2026-2029. $190M ❓ HASTE block-buy for DoD hypersonic test flights.
**Implication:** RKLB is the only vertically integrated launch + spacecraft provider at scale outside SpaceX. If Neutron flies Q4 2026 on schedule, the medium-lift market opens. Kill condition: Neutron delayed beyond Q2 2027 or loss of 2+ government contracts.
**Source:** GlobeNewswire May 2026, NASASpaceFlight Dec 2025

## ASTS FCC approval removes regulatory kill condition — carrier model is the moat vs Starlink

**Claim:** ASTS received FCC commercial authority April 22, 2026, removing its biggest regulatory overhang. Its carrier-partnership model (AT&T, Verizon) creates a distribution moat that SpaceX's consumer-direct Starlink cannot easily replicate — ASTS revenue flows through existing carrier billing relationships.
**Evidence:** BlueBird 6 demonstrated 120 Mbps to standard unmodified phones — largest commercial comms array in LEO. BlueBird 7 launched April 2026. Target 45-60 satellites by end-2026.
**Implication:** ASTS is now an operational execution story, not a regulatory story. The kill condition shifts to: Starlink wins a major carrier partnership, or ASTS launch cadence falls below 1/quarter. Valuation remains elevated — any miss triggers 30-50% drawdown.
**Source:** BusinessWire April 2026 (FCC), BusinessWire Dec 2025 (BlueBird 6)

## KTOS is T3's only true compounder — real revenue, Valkyrie scaling, Space Force diversification

**Claim:** Kratos Defense is the most fundamentally grounded name in T3: $1.35B 2025 revenue (+16.6% organic), 2026 guided $1.6-1.7B, with Valkyrie drone production ramping 8→40 units/year by 2027 and a $447M Space Force contract adding non-drone revenue.
**Evidence:** Profitable company with DoD contractual visibility. Valkyrie: USMC + Northrop Grumman $231.5M contract. Space Force: $447M missile warning/tracking contract. Counter-UAS: additional $7M contract.
**Implication:** KTOS is a defense-growth compounder with drone optionality — not a speculative bet. Lower risk than RKLB/ASTS/LUNR. Kill condition: Valkyrie program cancelled or DoD unmanned budget cut >20%.

## LUNR: $210M → $900M in one year is extreme execution risk tied to NASA budget

**Claim:** Intuitive Machines guided $900M-$1B 2026 revenue from $210M in 2025 — a 4-5x jump driven by NASA CLPS contracts, Lanteris acquisition, and new DoD contracts, but the entire thesis depends on NASA Artemis program budget remaining intact.
**Evidence:** $180M NASA task order March 2026. $943M backlog. 60-65% expected to convert in 2026. Positive Adjusted EBITDA guided (GAAP profitability unconfirmed ❓).
**Implication:** LUNR is a project-finance play, not a recurring-revenue business. If NASA faces Congressional budget cuts to lunar exploration, LUNR's revenue base collapses. First-mover on lunar infrastructure is a real option, but binary. Size accordingly — lottery-ticket allocation.
**Source:** StockTitan LUNR results, IBTimes $180M NASA contract, Simply Wall St

## BBAI: insufficient evidence for conviction — needs large sole-source win to differentiate from PLTR/Booz Allen

**Claim:** BigBear.ai operates in DoD AI analytics (decision support, adversary analysis) but competes directly against Palantir, Booz Allen Hamilton, and Leidos — all with larger balance sheets — and current contract evidence ($13.2M ORION, IDIQ vehicles) is too small to demonstrate a defensible niche.
**Evidence:** $13.2M sole-source DoD ORION contract (March 2025, 3.5 years). CDAO contract for foreign media AI analysis. SeaPort NxG IDIQ prime contractor vehicle. Revenue, cash burn, and balance sheet ❓ not confirmed.
**Implication:** BBAI is speculative tier within T3. Do not size above 1-2% NAV until a large sole-source win ($50M+) demonstrates it can compete with Palantir on DoD mission-critical systems. Flag for additional research.
