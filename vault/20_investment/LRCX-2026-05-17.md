## Business

  - **CSBG (Customer Support Business Group):** spare parts, upgrades, services — Q3 FY2026 ทำรายได้ $2B เป็นไตรมาสแรก (recurring, margin-rich)
- **ลูกค้าหลัก:** TSMC, Samsung, SK Hynix, Micron, Intel — top customers คือ memory + leading-edge logic fabs ทั้งโลก

## Moat / ความได้เปรียบในการแข่งขัน

1. **~80%+ share in advanced node plasma etch** (high-aspect-ratio, 60:1+ depth) — no competitive tool from AMAT or TEL can match LRCX at leading edge today
2. **"Content per wafer" compounds with each node transition:** GAA architecture = +15-20% etch steps vs FinFET; 3D NAND layer count 200→300+ = more etch per flat wafer start; LRCX earns more per wafer even with zero market share gain
3. **CSBG recurring revenue ($2B/quarter milestone Q3 FY2026):** installed base of 80,000+ tools generates annuity-like spares + services revenue, buffering WFE cyclicality
4. **Cryo 3.0 dielectric etch** — SEMI Award 2025 winner; becoming standard for advanced NAND used in AI storage

---

## Bull case

1. **GAA + HBM dual driver = automatic revenue per wafer compounding through 2027+** Intel + Samsung deploying 2nm GAA (etch-intensive) while HBM4/HBM5 era requires increasingly complex TSV etching — combined GAA+HBM revenue >$3B in 2025 with runway intact. LRCX earns more per wafer at every node even if wafer start volume is flat.
2. **WFE market guided $140B+ in 2026 (bias to upside) + LRCX SAM expanding mid-30% → high-30%** — share gain on expanding base = double compounding; Q3 FY2026 revenue +24% YoY confirms WFE supercycle is not pausing.
3. **Margin recovering faster than feared post-China controls: 49.9% Q3 vs guided 48.5%** — management's H2 2026 recovery thesis appears ahead of schedule; if China replacement materializes fully → margins return toward 50.6% record by FY2027.

---

## Bear case (steelman)

1. **China revenue -$600M headwind is only partially replaced:** Export controls cut China from 43% → <30% revenue. Management claims global multinational replacement by H2 2026 — but this is guidance, not proven. If replacement stalls and memory capex slows simultaneously → revenue misses Q4 FY2026 without China buffer.
2. **Valuation (~36x forward P/E) requires no capex pause:** At 36x forward P/E, any AI capex softening (even 1-2 quarters) triggers significant re-rating. Competitors AMAT (30x forward) and KLAC (37x forward) offer similar WFE exposure at lower multiples. April 2026 analyst downgrade already cited valuation + helium supply risk.
3. **AMAT + TEL gaining in target segments:** Applied Materials has broader portfolio and Morgan Stanley "top WFE pick 2026" designation. Tokyo Electron strong in Asia. If either closes technology gap in advanced etch for GAA or NAND → LRCX SAM expansion stops, creating structural multiple de-rating.
